## What are the timings for Shillong Teer?

The Shillong Teer results are typically announced twice a day, Monday to Saturday.
* **Round 1 (F/R)**: Usually between 3:45 PM and 4:30 PM.
* **Round 2 (S/R)**: Usually between 4:45 PM and 5:30 PM.
Please note that exact timings can occasionally vary due to weather or administrative reasons.

## Is Teer played on Sundays?

No, the official Shillong Teer games are not held on Sundays.

## What is a "Common Number" or "Target"?

A Common Number (or Target) is an estimated, high-probability number forecasted by players or analysts based on historical trends, previous results, or dream interpretations. It is a widely used term in the Teer community.

## Does Teer Club offer online betting?

**No.** Teer Club is strictly an information, analytics, and news platform. We do absolutely no real-money betting or payment processing. Our goal is to provide the fastest results and smartest tools for the Teer community.

## How do you calculate the Hot and Cold numbers?

Our algorithms analyze the last 30 to 90 days of official Teer results. "Hot" numbers are those that have appeared most frequently in recent draws, suggesting short-term momentum. "Cold" numbers are those that haven't been drawn in a significant period.

## Can I contribute to the Forum?

Yes! Teer Club aims to build the largest community of Teer enthusiasts. Creating an account is free, and you can share your predictions, discuss strategies, and talk with other members.
